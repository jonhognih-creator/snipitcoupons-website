FEATURES

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Snip It! does one thing brilliantly — keeps your coupons organised and ready to use.

Scan in seconds

Point your camera at any coupon. Snip It! reads the store name, discount, expiry date and barcode — no manual typing, ever.

Redeem with one tap

At the till, tap Redeem. Your barcode fills the screen for the scanner, and the coupon archives itself once used.

Never let one expire

Every coupon shows a live expiry countdown, and you get a reminder before a deal quietly dies in your wallet.

Store credits & gift cards too

Track balances as you spend them — that £25 John Lewis credit stays visible until you've used every penny of it.
