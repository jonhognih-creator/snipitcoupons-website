How it works

Three steps. That is it.

You do not need to learn anything. If you can take a photo, you can use Snip It!

1

Scan your coupon

Take a photo of any coupon — physical or digital. Our AI reads all the details automatically.

2

It lives in your wallet

The coupon is saved to your personal wallet, synced across all your devices, and sorted by expiry date.

3

Redeem when you are ready

Open the coupon, show the clean barcode or checkout code, tap Redeem. Done.
