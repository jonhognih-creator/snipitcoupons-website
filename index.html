Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Snip It! does one thing brilliantly — keeps your coupons organised and ready to use.

📷
Scan in seconds

Point your camera at any coupon. Our AI reads the store name, discount, expiry date and barcode — no manual typing, ever.

🎯
Redeem with one tap

At the till, tap Redeem. Your barcode fills the screen for the scanner. The coupon archives itself automatically once used.

⏰
Never let one expire

Every coupon shows a live expiry countdown. Urgent ones are highlighted so you never forget a deal sitting in your wallet.

How it works

Three steps to saving money

From paper coupon to redeemed in under a minute.

1
Take a photo

Open Snip It! and tap the camera button. Take a photo of any coupon, voucher, gift card or store credit slip.

2
AI does the work

Our AI extracts the store, discount amount, expiry date and barcode in seconds. Review and save with a single tap.

3
Tap Redeem at the till

When you're ready to pay, open the coupon and tap Redeem. Show the barcode, get your discount — done.
