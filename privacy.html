Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

We built Snip It! to be straightforward. We collect only what we need to make the app work, we do not sell your data, and you can delete your account and everything in it at any time.

Who we are

Snip It! is a coupon wallet application. When this policy says "we", "us" or "our", it refers to the team behind Snip It!. If you have any questions, email us at hello@snipit.app.

What information we collect

We collect information in two ways — what you give us directly and what the app generates automatically.

Information you provide:

  • Your email address when you create an account
  • Photos you take of your coupons
  • Coupon details (store name, discount, expiry date, barcode, checkout code)

Information collected automatically:

  • Your approximate location (only when the app is open and you have granted permission) — used solely to alert you when you are near a store you have a coupon for
  • Notification preferences you set inside the app
  • Basic usage information such as the date of your last scan — used only for rate limiting

How we use your information

  • To store and sync your coupons across your devices
  • To process coupon photos through our AI scanning feature (photos are sent to Anthropic's API for analysis and are not stored by Anthropic beyond the request)
  • To send you expiry reminders and nearby-store alerts if you have enabled those notifications
  • To allow you to share a coupon with someone else via a unique link

Third-party services we use

To provide the service we use the following third parties:

  • Supabase — stores your account information and coupon data. Data is stored securely and encrypted at rest. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic — processes coupon photos through the Claude AI API to extract coupon details. Photos are not retained after processing. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Foursquare — used to identify nearby stores when you have location enabled. Only your approximate coordinates are sent. Foursquare Privacy Policy

Location data

Location access is entirely optional. If you grant permission, the app uses your location while it is open to detect when you are near a store you have a coupon for. We do not store your location history. Your coordinates are sent to Foursquare to identify nearby venues and then discarded. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings, or turn off proximity alerts inside the app.

Push notifications

If you enable notifications, we send reminders when a coupon is about to expire and alerts when you are near a relevant store. We will not send you marketing messages, promotions unrelated to your own coupons, or any other unsolicited notifications.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with the third-party services listed above, and only to the extent necessary to operate the app.

If you use the coupon-sharing feature, the details of the coupon you share (store, discount, expiry, photo) are stored on our servers and accessible to anyone with the unique link. You are in control of which coupons you choose to share.

Data retention

Your data is kept for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your coupon data, account information and any shared coupons you created are deleted from our systems. Some information may remain in encrypted backups for up to 30 days before being permanently removed.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct or delete the personal data we hold about you. You can:

  • View and edit your coupon data at any time inside the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the Settings screen inside the app
  • Contact us at hello@snipit.app to request a copy of your data or ask any questions about how it is used

Children

Snip It! is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@snipit.app.
